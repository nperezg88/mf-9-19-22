About Me

I was born in Mexico, but moved to the US at a very young age. I grew up in the Central Valley in the beautifual state of California. At the age of 18 I joined the US Army specializing in logistics. Through the military I was able to travel around the US and the world. I am proud of my military service and become a US Citizen by the end of my term. The military afforded me many opportunities and benefits. I took advantage of these benefits and earned an Associate's degree in Business and a Bachelor's Degree in History. I've worked in many different industries ranging from fastfood, retail, sales, government sector and even served as a Wildland Firefighter for a season. More recently, as I've learned and practiced developing websites a new found passion and excitement has been ignited within me. For me it's the start of a new journey into the world of tech, and for the first time in a long time, I feel excited and hopefully of the opportunities this journey may bring.
